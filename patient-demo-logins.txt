Quick wiring checklist


_Layout.cshtml
Change the <body> tag to include the patient id (it’s safe on non-patient pages, it’ll just be empty):
<body data-patient-id="@User.FindFirst("PatientId")?.Value">



Make sure you’re including scripts in _Layout.cshtml in this order:
<script src="https://cdn.jsdelivr.net/npm/bootstrap@5.3.3/dist/js/bootstrap.bundle.min.js"></script>
<script src="https://cdn.jsdelivr.net/npm/chart.js"></script>
<script src="~/js/charts.js" asp-append-version="true"></script>
<script src="~/js/heatmap.js" asp-append-version="true"></script>
<script src="~/js/site.js" asp-append-version="true"></script>
@await RenderSectionAsync("Scripts", required: false)



Once you drop this site.js in and add the data-patient-id attribute, your patient dashboard should:


Play the heatmap as a video.


Update KPIs and the trend chart from the same CSV metrics.


Respect the user’s custom pressure threshold.


Let them leave comments tied to a session date.
